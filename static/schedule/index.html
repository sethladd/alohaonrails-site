Saturday, October 3, 2009

Rails Training in Paradise Installfest

Location: Ward Warehouse Conference Rooms - Upstairs

5:30pm : Doors Open
6pm - 9pm : Get your laptop configured for Ruby on Rails and the Rails Training in Paradise session to be held on Sunday. We’ll stay open until 9pm, but if your install is easy, you’ll get out early or you can stay to help others. We’ll have all the software ready to download. If you are attending Rails Training in Paradise, you must attend this session to ensure you laptop is ready to go. Dinner (Thai Food) and drinks will be provided!

Sunday, October 4, 2009

Rails Training in Paradise

Location: Ward Warehouse Conference Rooms - Upstairs

8:30am : Doors Open
9am - 4pm : Sarah Mei leads you through building, testing, and deploying your first Rails web application. Designed specifically for those new to Rails. After the training, you’ll be ready for the conference! We’ll break for lunch (not provided) and there are many options in the Ward Warehouse and Ward Center areas.
