Amazing Waikiki Hotel Deal, 4 Star for $79/night

Thursday, May 7th, 2009

Once your register for Aloha on Rails, you’ll probably need to find a hotel. One benefit of the economic downturn is that attendees to Aloha on Rails will be able to book some amazing hotel and airline deals.

For instance, we just found evidence of a 4 star Waikiki Hotel, the Hawaii Prince Hotel Waikiki, for a mere $79/night! Thanks to Beat of Hawaii for the heads up.


(A is the Conference Venue at the Marriott Waikiki and B is the Prince Hotel) View Larger Map

Granted, the Prince is 1.6 miles walking distance from the Conference venue (the Marriott Waikiki), but the weather is almost always perfect and a morning stroll past the beaches is a great way to start your day.

Regardless of where you’d like to stay for the conference, I hope this shows that getting a good deal for a Waikiki hotel room is possible. There are plenty of options in the area, and we’ll pass along any good deals when we find them. You’ll almost certainly find a good deal on a hotel room closer, or at, the Marriott Waikiki.

If you find a good deal for the conference, let us know and we’ll pass it along!
