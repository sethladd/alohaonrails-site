Cancellation Policy

If you must cancel for any reason you must notify us in writing by August 20th, for a refund less a $100 processing fee. Cancellations must be in writing by email. Cancellations within 45 days of the event are non-refundable. Confirmed and paid attendees who do not attend or who cancel after the deadline are liable for the entire fee. In the unlikely event of cancellation of the conference, the liability of Higher Order Function LLC is limited to the return of paid registration fees.
